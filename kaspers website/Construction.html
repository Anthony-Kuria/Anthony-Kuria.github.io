OSHA Compliant

In accordance with the Occupational Safety and Health Act 2007, we have made it our responsibility to provide protective gear and equipment including clothing and appliances. Where necessary, we provide suitable gloves (hand protection), footwear, goggles (face/eye protection) and head coverings to the workers, as type of hazard(s) dedicate.

Training:

Through our routine and mandatory tool-box-talk before and during project execution, We ensure that our teams are made aware of the prevailing risks associated with the current assignment. What they need to do/not do and the gear/equipment they must have to avoid the primary and secondary risks we have identified. We go to great lengths to avoid practices that may cause ill health or bodily injury to our employees. All our staff are properly trained and are required to work under the supervision of experienced leaders.

Tower Foundation Excavation & Reinforcement.

The construction of telecommunications towers require deep excavation, mechanical earth cutting equipment are used to reach the required width and depth. If the soil is loose or the excavation is deeper, we use some sort of shoring to support the sides from falling.

Tower Foundation Casting.

The foundation of any structure plays an important role in safety and satisfactory performance of the structure. The foundation main purpose is to safely transmits mechanical loads of the tower to the earth/ground. A transmission structure Without having a sound and safe foundation, cannot perform the functions for which it has been designed for.

Bunker Construction.

Telecom shelters protect communication equipment and operator network reliability from a variety of elements and threats, including extreme temperature changes, moisture, strong winds, fire, and intrusion among others. Our concrete shelters are engineered with flexibility in the design phase to allow for adaptation at the deployment site. The shelters can be concrete, steel, or include both steel and concrete construction elements in order to meet specific design requirements.
