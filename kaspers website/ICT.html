ICT INFRASTRUCTURE.

At BICATEL, we support businesses in;

  • Installation and commissioning of LAN and wLAN networks.
  • Trunking installations, Cable ways (trays) installations, and Structured cabling.
  • Data Center installations, (Tiers 1/2/3).
  • Installation of CCTV, Access Control and Alarm Systems.

Through our ICT infrastructure department, we act as the enabling link between IT services and preferred business technologies. We focus on processes with a goal of using proven, reputable processes to provide a stable operating environment for everyone using the provided technology. We ensure this is done through our four main objectives that is:

DESIGN & PLANNING:

To provide a lasting solution to our customers, we first source to establish the best viable and cost effective technology path for their organization. This is done through established processes of research, selection, introduction, maintenance, and sun-setting also known as productâ€™s life cycle.

DEPLOYMENT TECHNIQUE:

With the planning & design stage done and best technological path has been established, next comes deployment and With it, is the business risk expected. Our deployment process will ensure minimal impact to the business (change management), as we utilize established steps to control the introduction of hardware and software changes into your operating environment. These processes are also used to guide activities at both the production and test environments. Thus enabling our clients to minimize the impact on their system users and clients.

OPERATIONS MANAGEMENT:

Once the technology has been deployed, our management strategy is activated: Through our experienced and well trained personnel coupled with established systems we work to ease the challenges that may be experienced in the day to day running of the system. The target is crucial risk points like job scheduling, data management (including backup and recovery), and database administration. At BICATEL we prefer working with capacity planning tools, which enhances monitoring the health of current applications and infrastructure.

TECHNICAL SUPPORT:

This is our strongest point where our experienced and dedicated teams thrive. We systematically handle and document all the scheduled and unscheduled prevention/maintenance activities with surgical precision, solving most of the problems even before they arise. This being the last step of the ICT infrastructure implementation process and the main support to all technological stages earlier mentioned, we ensure that our customers get our best and most experienced technicians and engineers as their On-call technical support team.
